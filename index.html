About Second Chance

Founded in Yokohama, we're more than a moving company—we're sustainability advocates who believe every item deserves a second chance.

Since our founding, we've helped thousands of families and businesses across Kanto with reliable, bilingual moving and logistics services. Our team combines Japanese precision with international communication, ensuring smooth service for everyone.

We're committed to reducing waste through our reuse-first approach. Items are donated to local charities, sold to give them new life, or recycled responsibly through licensed partners. Every move is an opportunity to make a positive environmental impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on distance, volume, floors, elevator access, and special handling needs. Our instant estimator provides a range; exact quotes require photos/video of items.

Do you charge extra for no elevator?

Yes, stairs require additional labor. We add a fee per floor for buildings without elevators, disclosed upfront in your quote.

What can you dispose of?

Most household items, furniture, and appliances. Hazardous materials require special handling. We'll guide you through compliance for items like AC units and refrigerators.

Is appliance installation included?

Yes! Used appliance purchases include delivery, installation, and basic setup. Our 3-month warranty covers parts and labor.

Can you move on short notice?

We offer same-day service in Yokohama and Kanagawa (subject to availability). Contact us early morning for best chances.

Do you work weekends?

Yes, we operate 7 days a week, 8am-8pm. Weekend slots fill quickly, so book ahead when possible.

Are you licensed and insured?

Fully licensed for transport and waste disposal in Kanagawa. All moves are insured for damage or loss during transit.

Can I communicate in English?

Absolutely. Our team is bilingual (Japanese/English), with optional Arabic support. All documentation available in multiple languages.

Do you offer packing services?

Yes, we provide full or partial packing services. Materials (boxes, bubble wrap, tape) available for purchase or included in packing packages.

How do I track my quote request?

After submission, you'll receive confirmation via your preferred contact method (phone/email/LINE/WhatsApp) within 2 hours during business hours.
